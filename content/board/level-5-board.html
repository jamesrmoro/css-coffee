Now use align-items to move the coffee cups to the bottom corner of the table. This CSS property aligns items vertically and accepts the following values:

  • flex-start: Items align to the top of the container.
  • flex-end: Items align to the bottom of the container.
  • center: Items align to the vertical center of the container.
  • baseline: Items align to the baseline of the container.
  • stretch: Items stretch to fill the container.
.table {
	display: flex;
}
